List of what to do (Updated Quarterly)

  1. Plant Trees (50 points)
  2. Join Community Compost (30 points)
  3. Upcycle/Repurpose Something (10 points)
  4. Pick up Trash in your Neighborhood (15 points)
  5. Buy less non-perishable items (10 points)
  6. Plant herbs (Basil, Mint, etc.) for your kitchen, to avoid any unnecessary waste (20 points)
  7. Complete an Innerview (see sources) activity (activity will be analyzed and points will be assigned accordingly)
